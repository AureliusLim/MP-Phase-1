Assassin's Creed

Description

The Assassins and Templars have been at war for centuries. And now finally, with decades of preparation, the Templars have infiltrated the Assassin Brotherhood and torn them down from the inside. Now, Ethan must rebuild the Brotherhood and rise from the ashes of the Creed.

Genre

Open-World, RPG, Single-player, Assassin

Rating: 3/5

Unique story concept with the setting in the modern era. Interesting implementation of modern technplogy.
